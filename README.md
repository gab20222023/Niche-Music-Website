This is my Niche Music Website, also called: Niche-fy.
This website will let you explore and discover music you might never have heard of,
all while enhancing your listening experience.
As of the 13. of July 2026, this project is not completed, and in fact just starting out.
(This Project is actually for Hackclub #horizons, more info at https://horizons.hackclub.com/).
But I am working hard on it, and it should be ready in about one week if not earlier!

Motivation: I always have been a person who didn't listen mainstream Music. I was always more intrigued by older music from the 70s and 80s.
The reason for this project is mainly to help other people experience the wonder of listening to music that wasn't recommended by the algorithm, but rather something new, perhaps something even more exiting!
